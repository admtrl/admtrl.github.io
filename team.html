Principal Investigator

Dr. Clodualdo Aranas Jr., PEng (Associate Professor)

BSc Materials Engineering (University of the Philippines-Diliman), PhD Materials Engineering (McGill University)

Research Interests
  • Physical and mechanical metallurgy
  • Mechanical testing
  • Additive manufacturing
  • Hot deformation
  • Materials thermodynamics and phase transformation
  • Alloy design

Dr. Aranas is also managing the operations of the UNB Ballistics and Mechanical Test (BAM) Lab, a facility equipped with light-gas guns that can shoot projectile from subsonic to hypervelocity speeds.
